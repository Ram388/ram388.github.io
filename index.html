About

Born and raised in Miami, Florida, I grew to love photography from an early age. In 2017, I officially began my photography journey while on a family trip to St. Augustine, Florida with the help of the camera on my iPhone 5s.

Since then, I have gone from shooting and editing images on a cell phone, to honing my skills and developing into a creative and open minded photographer. I find that capturing the perfect moment still brings me the same kind of joy I had felt when I first started taking pictures years ago.

While I have practiced over the years and made drastic improvements, I still see myself as a kid with a camera, ready to observe and learn anything that comes my way. I am striving to develop my career in photography each day, as I aim to be the best photographer I can be, not only for myself but for those around me as well.
