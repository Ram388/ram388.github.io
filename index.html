Born and raised in Miami, Florida, I grew to love photography from an early age. 2017 was the official start to my photography journey, stemming from the camera on my iPhone 5s.

Since then, I have gone from shooting and editing images on my phone to honing my skills and developing myself into a creative and open minded photographer, up for any challenge that comes my way. I find that capturing the perfect moment still brings me the same kind of joy I had felt when I first started years ago.

While I have practiced over the years and made drastic improvements, I still see myself as a kid with a camera, ready to observe and learn anything that comes my way. I am striving to develop my career in photography more and more every day, as I aim to be the best photographer I can be, not only for myself but for those around me as well.
